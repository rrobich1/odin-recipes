Steak

Description

A simple and delicious recipe for steak.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Love

Steps

  1. Season steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Add oil to pan over high heat.
  3. Add steak to hot pan.
  4. Flip steak.
  5. Sear until cooked to perfection.
  6. Let rest, then garnish with love.
